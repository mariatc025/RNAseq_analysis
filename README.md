# RNAseq_analysis
This repository contains a complete RNA-seq reanalysis investigating the role of HPRT1 in breast cancer (J. Sedano et al. (2020)). The analysis is based on publicly available RNA-seq data (GEO: GSE149768) and explores HPRT1 expression across different breast cancer cell lines and knockdown conditions.

## Project overview
In this study, we analyze RNA sequencing data from three distinct breast cell lines: MCF-10A (non-tumorigenic), MCF-7 (estrogen receptor positive), and MDA-MB-231 (triple-negative). Each cell line was subjected to four treatment conditions: untreated, siControl, and two different HPRT1 knockdown conditions (siHPRT1-1 and siHPRT1-2), with two biological replicates per condition. The raw RNA-seq data for this analysis is available in the Gene Expression Omnibus (GEO) under accession GSE149768.

## Goals
Our analysis aims to:
- Characterize HPRT1 expression across different breast cancer subtypes
- Identify genes and pathways regulated by HPRT1 through differential expression analysis following HPRT1 knockdown
- Evaluate the potential of HPRT1 as a diagnostic and prognostic marker in breast cancer
- Understand the biological implications of HPRT1 upregulation in breast cancer progression
